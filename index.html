Dracula: King of Darkness

Act Structure

The tale of love, grief, and the darkness that answers when both are taken.

Prologue

The Pledge to Darkness

1489, Wallachia. Witness Vlad's fall from a noble prince to a hunter of souls after the brutal murder of his family. A love story becomes a deal with darkness.

Act II

Mina and the Look

In the sunlit archives of London, a reunion turns cold. Mina senses a recognition in the shadows — a memory of a past life as Elena.

Act IV

Mina Confesses

As reality fractures, Alecia Van Helsing makes the ultimate sacrifice to protect the man she silently loves. Nothing is as it appeared.

Act V

The Cruel Choice

The Cathedral collapses. Will you release Mina to her eternal love — or strike down the King of Darkness? Your ending belongs to you alone.

Dracula II: The Inheritance

The Story Behind the Story

London, 2026. A young artist. A three-hundred-year mission. A ritual that will take them back to where it all began.

Prologue · London 2026

The Road He's Always Drawn

Jonathan Harker III has filled sketchbook after sketchbook with a road he's never walked and a woman he's never met. Alecia Van Helsing knocks on his door. She knows why.

Acts I–III · Wallachia 1431

Arrival in the Cold

The ritual takes them to a frozen plain beneath a fortress Jonathan has drawn from dreams for nine years. A household. A voivode. A priest whose warmth is never quite warm enough.

Act IV · Adrianople 1442

The Ottoman Court

Young Vlad and Radu are sent as hostages to the Ottoman Empire. Jonathan and Alecia follow — watching a boy either become the most dangerous man in Europe, or remain entirely himself.

Act V · Wallachia 1447

What the Demon Costs

Sixteen years of observation. Forty-seven half-seconds of cold in a corridor. The shape beneath the losses becomes visible. The pattern is now undeniable. The chain can be broken.

Act VI · London 2026

The Return

Back in the underground room where they started, with the chalk circles faded and London light through the crack in the ceiling. The history has changed. The grief hasn't. One question remains.

Post-Credits

Not Luck

Vlad and Elena's modern flat. Evening. He's cooking badly. She's correcting him. She tells him about a dream — two people, one drawing. "It made me think of you." He says: "Not luck. Something more reliable."

Dracula II · Act VI

Three Endings. Each One Earns It.

After 38 chapters and sixteen years inside the Drăculești household, you return to London with one question left. What do you do with what you know?

A

The Quiet Life

Keep the story between you. Seal the archive. Build a life in the house where the mission used to live. The story is real whether it is shared or not.

"Some things keep finding the right hands."

B

The Archive

Write it all down. The demon's method. The forty-seven moments of cold. Instructions addressed to whoever comes after — or to nobody, which is the better outcome.

"Also: look after each other. Non-negotiable."

C

Find Them

Locate Vlad and Elena in this life — ordinary people, no memory of anything. Watch them on a bench in Kensington Gardens. A book. An argument. A dog with opinions. Walk away.

Alive. Together. Home.

All three endings share the same epilogue: the history books rewritten, Vlad and Elena in Kensington Gardens, the ritual room empty at last. The choice is what you did with the story — not whether the story worked.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection and Usage

Interactive Stories: Sky ENG (including Dracula: King of Darkness and Dracula II: The Inheritance) is designed to respect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal identity data — such as names, phone numbers, or email addresses. Your narrative choices and game progress are stored locally on your device only.

2. Advertising and Tracking

To support free development, we display third-party advertisements. Ad partners may use device identifiers (such as Apple's IDFA) to provide personalized advertisements and analyse ad performance. You can manage your tracking preferences through your device settings or the App Tracking Transparency prompt shown on launch.

3. Third-Party Services

Aside from advertising partners, we do not share any data with third-party analytics or cloud services. We do not maintain user profiles on external servers. No data from either game is transmitted beyond what the ad network requires.

4. Contact and Support

If you contact our support team via email, we use your contact information solely to resolve your issue. We do not sell or use this information for marketing purposes. Contact us at: codelovin@outlook.com
